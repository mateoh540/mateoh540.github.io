Prototyping

I first assembled the hardware components on the breadboard and began developping the software to establish Bluetooth communication with the Dexcom G7 device and fetch glucose monitor data including the reading, trend, and timestamp. I then used a 7-segment display, OLED display and LEDs to display the current reading, the trend, time since the reading, and a status indicating in-range (green), high (yellow), and low (red).
