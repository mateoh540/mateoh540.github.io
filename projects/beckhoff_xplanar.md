## Beckhoff XPlanar 6DOF Position Measurement

## Goal
Measure the 6DOF pose of an XPlanar mover using stereovision

## 
DEvelopped algorithms in TwinCAT PLC to preserve real-time compatibility. 

## Implementation
Stereovision algorithms, twincat vision, LED mounted frame for detection. 

## Results
\micro m translational accuracy, mrad rotational accuracy.
